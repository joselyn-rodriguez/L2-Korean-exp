Headphone Check

Before beginning the experiment you will perform a short test to ensure you are wearing headphones. if you are not wearing headphones, please put some on now. You will not be able to complete the experiment if you are not wearing headphones.

Now, please calibrate your audio level for the check by playing the following sound and setting your volume. First, set your computer volume to about 25% of maximum. Press the button, then turn up the volume on your computer until the calibration noise is at a loud but comfortable level. Play the calibration sound as many times as you like.

Please continue if you are able to hear the audio at comfortable volume. The volume should be loud enough to hear clearly.
